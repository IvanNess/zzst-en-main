ZigZag Studio
A video studio for your startup.
We are a production studio in Belarus, specializing in the creation of marketing videos for startups and IT projects. We make state-of-the-art videos to fulfill all your goals.

We have a team of talented and diligent people, who love their work and value their clients, and will do everything to make working with us easy and pleasurable.

TYPES OF VIDEO FOR YOUR PROJECT

EXPLAINER VIDEOS

Do you have a great idea, but it's difficult to explain? We simplify your difficulties. With the aid of our explainer video, your idea will become crystal clear to potential users, leaving them unable to resist!

TUTORIAL VIDEOS

Tutorial videos are an excellent way to win over and keep clients. Easy video instructions will make your product convenient to use, and allow clients to evaluate all its strengths.

VIDEOS FOR CROWDFUNDING AND PRESENTATIONS FOR INVESTORS

Do you need money to implement your idea? A video presentation will demonstrate the uniqueness and potential of your idea to investors, and a good video will increase the success of your crowdfunding campaign.

VIDEO CONTENT

Useful articles and interesting reviews — we can use any content to make a video[SM2] which you can use to attract clients and earn their trust, and to gain prestige.

VIDEO COMMERCIALS

Is it time to think about the awareness and popularity of your product? Marketing and viral videos will attract popular attention and increase sales.

CORPORATE VIDEOS

Congratulatory videos and video reports of events will draw your project team together and make it more productive.

OUR SERVICES

ANIMATED VIDEOS

Eye-catching videos containing colourful characters and infographics provide information in an accessible and captivating form. They are very popular throughout the world and fantastic for explainer videos and video presentations.

LIVE ACTION VIDEOS

Enthralling live action videos are particularly attractive to viewers. Despite being more difficult to produce, they generate greater trust, and produce a greater emotional impact, making them suitable for ads and explainer videos.

SCREENCASTS

Grabbing a video from a computer screen is the simplest way to tell people how to use your product. Enhancing the video with voice-overs and colourful graphics will produce quality[SM4] tutorial and explainer videos.

LANGUAGE SUPPORT

We provide language support for your videos (voice-overs, subtitles), enabling you to enter the world market and reach out to vast numbers of users.

VIDEOS FROM PRESENTATIONS

Do you want to make a video for your project with minimal expense? We can convert your slide presentation into an animated video. This is the simplest and cheapest means of creating the first video for your project.

VIDEO PROMOTION

Existing tools for video promotion yield great results in a short time. We choose the best promotion strategy according to your aims and resources.

TEXT WRITING

Information must be supplied intelligibly and interestingly, especially texts for websites and presentations. We write texts of any complexity according to your needs.

TRANSLATIONS

We also provide translation services into foreign languages. All our translation work is carried out and edited by native speakers, so you need not be concerned for your reputation on the world market.

PHOTOGRAPHY

Professional photographs of your prototype or team will create a more vivid picture of your project, and will greatly enhance your website or presentation.
